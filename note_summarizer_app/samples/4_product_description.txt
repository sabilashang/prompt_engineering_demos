Audio Quality: Featuring advanced 12mm dynamic drivers and support for AAC and aptX codecs, the TechFlow Pro delivers rich, detailed sound with deep bass, clear mids, and crisp highs. The adaptive EQ automatically adjusts audio output based on your ear shape and listening environment, ensuring optimal sound quality in any situation.

Active Noise Cancellation: Our proprietary ANC 4.0 technology uses six microphones to detect and cancel up to 98% of ambient noise. Whether you're on a busy subway, in a crowded office, or traveling by plane, enjoy your music without distractions. Smart Features: Touch controls allow easy management of playback, calls, and voice assistants. Automatic pause/play when removing or inserting earbuds. Multipoint connectivity lets you seamlessly switch between two devices. The companion app offers customizable EQ settings, firmware updates, and find-my-earbuds functionality.
